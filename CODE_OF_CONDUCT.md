# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment
for everyone involved in the MindTrace AI project.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate
- Provide constructive feedback
- Respect privacy and boundaries

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Sharing private or sensitive data
- Unethical or harmful AI practices

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove contributions
that violate this code of conduct.
